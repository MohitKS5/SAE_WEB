About Our Alumni

For the past few years, alumni have provided guidance and have imparted very crucial information thus helping us inch closer to our goal. Many alumni have come forward exceeding our expectations and have helped us achieve the current position which we are in. But the project, still being in its developing stage requires all the help that it can get. The help and contribution that we get from our alumni is of utmost importance in nurturing our project and honing the skills of our team. Contribution in terms of parts, training or exploring industrial contacts would help us immensely in strengthening our position.

A donation of any amount is greatly appreciated.

Any “unconditional” donation made will get 125% tax exemption. For this donor has to send a letter to the institute along with cheque [in name of “Registrar IITK”, payable at “Kanpur”] stating he is donating the amount unconditionally to IITK for “Formula SAE Project”.
